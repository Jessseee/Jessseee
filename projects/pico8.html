PICO-8

Lately I felt inspired by the limitations of the 8-bit fantasy console PICO-8. Made by Lexaloffle Games this small arcade machine that never existed brings out a type of nostalgia and boosts creativity with its limited specs.

One of the reasons I stumbled upon this fun engine is because of the awesome game Celeste. The game was originally developed using PICO-8 and later remade in a more capable game engine to be released on consoles and PC. The original PICO-8 game can still be played on an old pc hidden in the game's levels.

Cartridges

Connect Four

Life

Conway’s game of life. Including a simple but beautiful pattern that stabilizes after 450 cycles. A tribute to the brilliant John Conway who died in 2020.
